# Viesta Nutrition — About

## Brand identity

- **Public storefront brand:** Viesta Nutrition
- **Registered legal business:** Viesta Health Nutrition
- **Primary market:** Kenya

Customer-facing brand content should use `Viesta Nutrition`. Contracts, policies, privacy-controller disclosures, copyright notices, and other formal legal references should use `Viesta Health Nutrition`.

## Overview

Viesta Nutrition is a Kenyan health and wellness brand helping individuals and families make more informed everyday choices through supplements, personal care products, wellness options, clearer product information, and attentive customer support.

The storefront presents a considered product range and a WhatsApp-based order-request flow. It does not diagnose conditions, prescribe treatment, guarantee product outcomes, or replace advice from a qualified healthcare professional.

## Mission

To empower people to make more informed wellness choices through considered products, clearer information, and attentive support.

## Vision

To become one of East Africa's most trusted health and wellness brands, recognized for quality, integrity, innovation, and customer care.

## Core product areas

- Health supplements
- Blood sugar support
- Blood pressure support
- Joint and mobility care
- Men's health
- Women's wellness
- Weight management
- Beauty and skincare
- Vision support
- Immunity products

Category names describe storefront organization and customer discovery. They must not be presented as evidence that a product diagnoses, treats, prevents, or cures a medical condition.

## Content principles

- Use conservative, supportable descriptions.
- Prefer `quality-led`, `considered`, and `thoughtfully selected` over unverified premium claims.
- Do not claim that ingredients are natural, sustainably sourced, safe, or effective without approved evidence supporting the specific product and wording.
- Keep product education general and direct customers to product labels and qualified professional advice where appropriate.
- Preserve visible confirmation states for product ingredients, directions, warnings, labels, and claims until business and regulatory review is complete.
