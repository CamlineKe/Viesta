# Viesta Health Shop — About

## Overview
Viesta Health Shop is a Kenyan health and wellness brand focused on helping individuals and families improve their wellbeing through quality health, beauty, and wellness products. The company emphasizes offering premium supplements, personal care products, and natural wellness solutions that support healthier lifestyles.

## Mission
To empower people to take control of their health by providing safe, effective, and natural wellness solutions.

## Vision
To become East Africa's most trusted health and wellness brand, recognized for quality, integrity, innovation, and customer care.

## Core Highlights

- Premium-quality supplements and wellness products
- Focus on natural and sustainably sourced ingredients
- Health supplements
- Blood sugar support
- Blood pressure support
- Joint & mobility care
- Men's health
- Women's wellness
- Weight management
- Beauty & skincare
- Vision support
- Immunity products
- Commitment to helping customers make informed wellness decisions through trusted products and customer education
