# Viesta Inventory

This document is the single source of truth for Viesta storefront inventory, product images, pack formats, retail offers, and pricing status.

The storefront serves retail customers. Minimum order quantities and wholesale prices do not apply.

Price status:

- **Confirmed**: the retail price and offer have been verified against `architecture/Viesta_Retail.md`.
- **Unconfirmed**: no verified retail price is available. The storefront must show the product as unconfirmed and must not use a wholesale or placeholder price.
